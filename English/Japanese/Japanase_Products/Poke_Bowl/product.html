Poke Bowl

Savor the freshness of diced, raw fish—typically tuna or salmon—accentuated by a medley of colorful vegetables, avocado, and a drizzle of savory soy sauce. The bowl is then elegantly topped with sesame seeds and seaweed, creating a symphony of textures and tastes in every bite.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh diced fish (tuna or salmon)
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Avocado
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame seeds
  • Seaweed
